About Me

I am a San Diego Native and a passionate creative that enjoys expressing themself through languages, music, and art. I am a prolific painter, musician, and an aspiring audio engineer.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Music recording and audio design with a minor in French. I am expected to graduate from SDSU in the fall of 2023.

During my time at SDSU, I have cultivated my skills in recording, production, mixing, vocal editing, sound design, and music composition. Most recently, I have been working live events, gaining experience in sound reinforcement, sound check and live mixing.
