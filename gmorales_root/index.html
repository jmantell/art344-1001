About Me

My passion in life is drawing. I began drawing rather late in my life, nearing the end of my senior year of high school. Then in my second year of college I realized that I wanted to go into a career involing art and drawing. At this moment I am working towards a career in animation.

I am an avid enjoyer of video games. I have been playing video games since I was elementary. I believe the first video game I played was Chicken Little on the GameBoy Advanced, which I still have to this day.

My favorite sport is volleyball. Although for a majority of my life I had mainly played soccer, during college was when I discovered volleyball.
