About Me

My name is Ashley Ordaniel, I was born and raised in San Diego. I'm currently a multimedia major at San Diego State and in my last semester here. I transferred from Southwestern College. If things go right, I'll hopefully be able to graduate this semester.

My favorite color is blue, but I wear black most of the time. Some of my favorite shows are Freaks and Geeks and Twin Peaks. I'm much more of a night owl than an early bird.

I prefer tea over coffee most of the time. My school schedule is quite busy this semester but, I like finding and collecting vinyls in my free time or finding new cafes in the area.
