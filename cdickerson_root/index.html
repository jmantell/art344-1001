About Me

I am a bilingual, third year Graphic Design student who owns a small art business called Raccatsuko. My favorite hobbies include livestreaming video games such as Legend of Zelda (Ocarina of Time and Majora's Mask) on TikTok, working out at the gym, and drawing. I have a very short attention span and most likely have ADHD.

As I mentioned, I have a strong relationship with art and design. I've been an artist all my life, so I decided, "Why not make a living for it?" As of now, my business consists of me accepting commissions and making designs to print out stickers and then sell them. I've drawn a variety of things; from humans, animals, objects, backgrounds, and so on, and I'd like to consider myself a good artist. My Instagram page where I post my artworks and personal projects is @raccatsuko.

If there's anything I love more in this world, it's cats and the Legend of Zelda series. I love Zelda so much that I make fan art of it. I previously mentioned how I livestreamed, and on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays, I livestream Zelda games on TikTok. If you wanna check that out, my Tiktok account is @raccocaine. I've gain over 3,000 followers and received a good amount of donations from my followers and viewers.
